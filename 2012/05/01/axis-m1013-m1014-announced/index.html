Axis M1013 & M1014 Announced for Q2 2012

Tue, May 1, 2012

Tweet

Axis has announced two updates to their M10 network camera series, the M1013 & M1014. According to the press release, the M1013 will offer SVGA resolution, while the M014 will have “HDTV 720p”, each equipped with H.264 compression. Though no release date has been announced, they’ll be available some time in Q2 2012. For more information, you can see the press material on each cameras on the Axis website.
