Wondering what to do with your old iPhone? Brush off the dust and try the new SensrCam Pro app; it turns your iOS device into a network camera able to upload images to Sensr.net! From the app description:

Download SensrCam Pro and transform your iOS device into an IP surveillance camera. Sign up for a free account on Sensr.net, enter your camera information into the app, and hit play to start uploading images to the Sensr.net servers. 

Your images can be viewed from anywhere around the world. From Sensr.net you can review your archive and make clips to share with friends.

Using an iPhone? SensrCam Pro can live broadcast images over cellular networks too. Capture footage from the road, walking at the park, or at social events.

Have an older iOS device laying around? Repurpose it with Sensrcam Pro. Netcams can cost hundreds of dollars. A repurposed iPhone with SensrCam Pro is cheap, and the image quality can’t be beat.

Possible uses:

▶Monitor your home or pet while your at work

▶Car accident documentation

▶Real estate scouting

▶Mobile/Portable surveillance

▶Dash camera

▶Live blogging

SensrCam Pro uploads 640×480 or 320×240 images to Sensr.net at 1, 6, or 30 images per minute or 1, 2, or 5 images per second.





There are an incredible amount of uses for SensrCam Pro, but if you get tired of holding up your iPad all day, iOmounts are your new best friend. They have three variations you can see in the photos above — the iOstand, iOmini, and iOwall, and they’re a great pair with SensrCam Pro.

Thanks to John from Logic Consulting LLC for creating the app. We’re looking forward to seeing more great third party Sensr.net apps in the future.

Requirements: Compatible with iPhone, iPod touch (4th generation), iPad 2 Wi-Fi, iPad 2 Wi-Fi + 3G, iPad (3rd generation) and iPad Wi-Fi + 4G. Requires iOS 4.0 or later.
