Tenvis Mini319W: NetCam Deal of the Day

Tue, Mar 27, 2012

Tweet

The Tenvis Mini319W is on sale @ Amazon for $63. It’s a great entry level network camera for the price, equipped with wifi, night vision, a 300k pixel CMOS, two way audio, FTP upload, 640×480 resolution, and several other features. It works great on Sensr.net, and hey, it even comes in three colors — white, silver, and black — to fulfill all your stylish needs. You can find more details on the camera’s specs on the Tenvis website.
