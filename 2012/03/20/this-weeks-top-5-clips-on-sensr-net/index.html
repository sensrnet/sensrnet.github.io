There have been some amazing shots captured on Sensr.net lately, and we figured it’d be great to post a top 5 clips list every week, pointing out some of the really wonderful time-lapses that have been posted this week. Thanks to all of our awesome users!

5. TARDIS Cam

_Ydrolina’s Cam_

__Fans of the British television series, Doctor Who, will enjoy Ydrolina Cam’s view of their backyard TARDIS. Very cool!

**4. Eiffel Tower from Day to Night

** _EiffelTowerCam_

Though it’s only three frames, it’s a great view of the Eiffel Tower going from day into night. Check our more on the live view here.

3. Dinner & Nap

_Josie’s Camera_

Josie  is a cute new pup on Sensr.net! She’s frequently up to something funny, so make sure to check up on her live view often!

**2. Beautiful View

** _meteyobanyeres webcam2_

__The Meteyo Banyeres Webcam shows a beautiful view from a weather station in Spain. For more information, check out their site here: http://www.meteobanyeres.com/.

**1. New Puppies and their Mom, Tulip

** _Tulip Cam_

Coming in at number one this week, the TulipCam has been extremely popular in the short time she’s been on Sensr.net. She just gave birth to four new puppies at the Tails to Tell Animal Rescue Shelter in Alberta, Canada! For more information on Tails to Tell and Tulip, check out their website, http://tailstotell.ca.
