Record Sharx 2700W with Sensr.net

Tue, Dec 11, 2012

Tweet

Sharx Security has been making quality security cameras for a number of years.  Their high quality cameras have great image quality and are very stable.  They also work great with Sensr.net.

To add a Sharx camera to Sensr.net, you just have to log into Sensr.net and get the FTP credentials for your camera.  Then go to the camera’s interface and setup an FTP server with those credentials.

Once you get the server setup, tell the camera to push to Sensr.net by enabling the FTP periodic sending.  We recommend sending one frame per second. The configuration looks like this.

[][3]

After that, your camera should show up in your Sensr.net camera list.  Here’s a month view from my office camera.
