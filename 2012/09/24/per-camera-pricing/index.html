Unified Features

We’ve decided to simplify our plans.  All plans now come with one month of storage, alerts, mobile access, guest invitations, and a lot more.

Per Camera Pricing

Our subscriptions are now based on the number of cameras.  They start at $9.95 per month for the first IP camera and additional cameras are only $4.95 per camera.   Try out the slider on our plans page to see how much it will cost you for different numbers of cameras.

Annual Options

To save even more you can signup for an annual plan, $99 per year for the first camera, $49 per year for additional cameras.

Free Accounts

****Guest accounts are still free, but if you want to connect your own camera, you’ll have to pay.

Risk Free, Cancel Any Time

If you’re not happy with the service we’ll give you a full refund if you cancel in the first month.   Just contact support and we’ll make it happen.
