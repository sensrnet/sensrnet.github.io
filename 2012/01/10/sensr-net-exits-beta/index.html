The plans will be per user and based mainly on how much data you want to upload and save on Sensr.net. Here’s the basic idea, we’ll be updating the site soon with all the details.

Free – 500 images per day, 3 day rewind.
Slim ($3/mo) – 1k images per day, 1 week rewind.
Sensible ($6/mo) – 5k images per day, 2 week rewind.
Stellar ($9/mo) – 10k images per day, 4 week rewind.
Supreme ($29/mo) – 100K images per day, infinite rewind.
