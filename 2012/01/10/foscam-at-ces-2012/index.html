Tue, Jan 10, 2012

Tweet

And they’re unveiling some really awesome new models at this year’s Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas! If you’ve made it to Vegas for the event this year, you can go visit the Foscam team at booth 21931, South Hall Level 1.

To sweeten the deal a little more, Foscam is having a special CES 2012 sale at www.foscam.us, www.foscam.ca, and www.foscam.co.uk to celebrate the event! If you use the coupon code “CES2012” you’ll receive the discount.
