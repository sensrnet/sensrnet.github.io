How to Setup Region of Interest (ROI) on Sensr.net

Wed, Apr 25, 2012

Tweet

Check out a cool new clip that shows off the region of interest feature on Sensr.net, allowing users to easily see where motion has been detected by framing the motion with a red box.

Turning ROI on is extremely easy! Login to Sensr.net, go to “My Cameras,” and choose your camera.

Once there, click on “settings,” as seen blow.

Click on the “Motion Detection” tab and move the Regions of Interest slider to “ON.” You must have sensr.net motion detection on low, medium, or high to see ROIs.

Click “Update Camera.” Now you will be able to see all future motion events highlighted by a red box!

If you have any questions about region of interest, please feel free to ask in the comments.
