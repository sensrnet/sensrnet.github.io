Setting the Timezone on Your Sensr.net Camera

  • October 28, 2011
  • Adam Beguelin
When you add a camera to Sensr.net we ask for your timezone, but sometimes folks forget to set it. It defaults to America/Los Angeles because that’s the home time zone of Sensr.net. If you want to change it, go to your camera page and click on the little gear in the upper right. Here’s a picture where I’ve circled the gear in red. This gear opens the settings panel.
