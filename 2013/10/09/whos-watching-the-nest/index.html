Mark, a Sensr.net user from Michigan, setup a camera in his boat port to watch a robin’s nest. He soon found out that he was not the only one watching.  Mark reports that everyone made it safely from the nest.  William, the family cat, didn’t catch any birds.

Cat watches Robin from boat. Sensr.net keeps an eye on both.

Mark uses the Foscam 8910 to connect to Sensr.net. In fact, he has three cameras connected to our service.  Here’s what he has to say about it:

I use Sensr.net because of my job, I travel often. It’s not really for security but rather so I can feel like I am home. Often my wife waves, or hold up things she finds. Sensr.net is very important to me, more important than you may know. Here she is holding a snake she caught in the utility room behind her.

I may be a thousand miles away, but I look often and feel at home.  — Mark B

Mark’s wife likes to wave to him over their Sensr.net cameras.
