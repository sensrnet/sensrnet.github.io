How to Setup The HooToo HT-IP006N With Cloud Recording

  • July 30, 2014
  • Geoffrey Pottow

HooToo outdoor model

The HooToo HT-IP006N is the outdoor camera model produced by HooToo, the company that made the camera I wrote about the other day. Like this other camera the HooToo HT-IP006N has a number of top of the line features and a very accessible UI. This one is more expensive than the last one coming in at $299. It is no small purchase but gets great reviews on amazon so it is definitely worth consideration. Of  course the best part about this camera is that it is compatible with Sensr.net. This tutorial will show you how to get your camera setup and recording to the cloud.
