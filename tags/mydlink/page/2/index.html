Setup D-Link DCS-5020L Recording with FTP Uploads HOWTO

  • January 31, 2014
  • Adam Beguelin

DCS-5020L from D-Link

The D-Link DCS 5020L is one of their more popular models of camera.  It has night vision, pan and tilt, WiFi and it works with the mydlink service.  The DCS 5020L is also pretty affordable at around $135 on Amazon these days.

Recording Needed

While the MyDlink service will let you view this camera remotely, it doesn’t support any kind of recording or saving of images. That’s where Sensr.net comes in.  We’ll save up to a month of archives automatically.  This blog post will show you how to setup the DCS-5020L so you can see what happened while you weren’t looking!

I’ll also show you how to push images to Sensr.net so you can take advantage of all of our cool features, SMS and email alerts, 30 days of archiving, no firewall config, clip sharing, embedable live streams, guest access, etc.
