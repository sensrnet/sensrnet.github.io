The Great Pacific Race on Sensr.net

  • June 24, 2014

Tweet

The biggest, baddest human endurance competition on the planet is sharing a camera on sensr.net! The Great Pacific Race is an ocean rowing race from Monterey, California to Honolulu, Hawaii. The competitors have no sails or motors, only their bodies to row them across the biggest ocean in the world! The boats left on June 7th but as they were gearing up and preparing for their journey the Great Pacific Race setup a camera and shared the staging area with the world!
