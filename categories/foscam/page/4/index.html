New Foscam FI8910W Available on Amazon

  • February 1, 2012
  • Robert Whitefield
Foscam’s new FI8910W is available on Amazon for about $100. While similar to its predecessor, the FI8918W, the FI8910W has a very significant upgrade: it’s designed to mitigate the loss of daytime color accuracy that plagues most IR night vision network cameras. Using an “embedded IR-cut filter,” the FI8910W claims to provide true and accurate images, fixing the problems related to washed out and discolored images.
