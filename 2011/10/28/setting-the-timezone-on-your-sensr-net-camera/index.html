Setting the Timezone on Your Sensr.net Camera

Fri, Oct 28, 2011

Tweet

When you add a camera to Sensr.net we ask for your timezone, but sometimes folks forget to set it. It defaults to America/Los Angeles because that’s the home time zone of Sensr.net.

If you want to change it, go to your camera page and click on the little gear in the upper right. Here’s a picture where I’ve circled the gear in red.

This gear opens the settings panel.

After you open the settings panel, you click the “Time Zone” pulldown under Camera Timezone and you can change the timezone for your camera. Now we’ll use that timezone when creating views for your camera, including the timestamp on the live view.

Here’s a shot of the timezone setting:

The red circle shows where to click to edit your camera's timezone.
