The super inexpensive Wansview Wireless PTZ/Night Vision IP cam is today’s network camera deal of the day.  For only $60, you get quite a few features. It’s got a standard 300k pixel sensor (1⁄4 inch CMOS), full PTZ, night vision, wifi, audio, and works great with Sensr.net! It’s basically a [Foscam FI89189W][3] clone, but hey – why not test it out for the price? And it’s got pretty good reviews on Amazon, too!
