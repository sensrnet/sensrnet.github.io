New user editable camera fields.

You can now add information about your [sensr.net][1] camera. Go to the DVR page for your][1] camera and simply click to edit the fields below the camera images.

  • Description
  • Model
  • Homepage
  • Location

    • Why should you add this info?  It will make it easier for folks to find your camera using the [sensr.net][1] search field. If you share your camera with your friends, it will also let your friends know more about your camera. If you share your camera with the world, it will let others find your camera more easily.

    For instance, you can search for all the cameras in Incline Village and you’ll get these three cameras.  As more and more folks add camera info, we’ll be able to surface more interesting search results on sensr.net and your camera will get more views!

    Search results for 'Incline Village'

    You’ll need to be logged in before you can edit the camera information for your camera.  And of course, you can’t edit the camera information for other users’ cameras.  We also support comments, if you have something to say about another user’s camera.  But we’ll save that for another blog post.
