LOFTEK CXS 2200: Network Camera Deal of the Day

Thu, Sep 15, 2011

Tweet

LOFTEK’s CXS 2200 is on sale for $71 right now, normally priced around $100. While we haven’t played with one ourselves yet, the camera boasts high-def resolution, high-quality audio, night vision up to 15 feet, and it’s wireless. There are plenty of features for the price, making it our network camera deal of the day! It’s also got 4 stars out of 47 reviews on Amazon, which is pretty rare for the less expensive network camera models.

I think I’ll pick one up this week to test it out. Expect instructions on how to set up the LOFTEK CXS 2200 and a mini-review in the coming days. If you’ve got a CXS 2200 already, let us know what you think about it in the comments, and get it set up on [Sensr.net][3] so you can share it with the world!
