Asante Securenet Voyager 2: Network Camera Deal of the Day

Fri, Sep 16, 2011

Tweet

The Asante Securenet Voyager 2 is on sale for $130 on Newegg, normally priced between $250 and $170.

We only recently discovered this camera at [Sensr.net][3], so we haven’t had a chance to test one ourselves, though we probably will soon because the features are pretty impressive for the price. It has a 1.3 megapixel sensor, wide angle lens, PTZ, audio, and it’s wireless.

Cons? Though it claims to have night vision, several reviews have said it barely works at all, but captures decent video at night if the area is well lit. We’ll let you know what we think and write up a how-to when we pick one up. If you’ve got any Asante network cams, let us know how you’ve liked yours in the comments!
